Mississpi Pot Roast

Description

This recipe is dead simple to make and it tastes delicious. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • 3 lb chuck roast
  • 10 pepperoncinis (plus some of the juice from the jar)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1 package buttermilk ranch dressing mix

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours.
  2. Remove the meat from the slow cooker, shred, return to the slow cooker. Serve by spooning meat and juices together.
