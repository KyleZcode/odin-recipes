Mexican Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This dish is easy to make, and usually makes enough for leftovers the next night, two big advantages for me. There is plenty of flavor, and some sour cream or hot sauce can also help change things up.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/4 cup onion, diced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can green chiles, 4 oz.
  • 1 can black beans, 15.5 oz.
  • 1 bag frozen corn, 14.4 oz.
  • 1 can red enchilada sauce, 10 oz.
  • 3 cups shredded chedar & Moterey jack cheese
  • 4 cups frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 375 degrees. Either spray a 9x13 baking dish with non-stick spray, or line with non-stick foil
  2. Cook the ground beef and onion thoroughly.
  3. Add taco seasoning, green chiles, black beans, frozen corn, and the enchilada sauce to the meat and onion, stir until combined. Cook over medium heat for another 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Add 2 cups of shredded cheese to the meat mixture and stir until blended. Pour into the baking dish and spread evenly. Place the tater tots evenly over top. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven, add the remaning cup of cheese over the top. Bake another 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
